Question 2

Who was the first President of the United States?

Answer

George Washington was the first President of the United States, serving from 1789 to 1797. He played a crucial role in the American Revolution and helped establish many of the traditions and precedents of the presidency.

Question 3

Which event marked the beginning of World War II?

Answer

World War II began with Germany's invasion of Poland on September 1, 1939. This act led to Britain and France declaring war on Germany, marking the official start of the global conflict.

Question 4

Who wrote the Declaration of Independence?

Answer

Thomas Jefferson was the primary author of the Declaration of Independence, which was adopted by the Continental Congress on July 4, 1776. The document declared the thirteen American colonies' independence from British rule.

Question 5

Which ancient civilization developed the concept of zero?

Answer

The concept of zero was developed by ancient Indian mathematicians around the 5th century CE. This revolutionary mathematical concept was later transmitted to the Islamic world and then to Europe, fundamentally changing mathematics.
