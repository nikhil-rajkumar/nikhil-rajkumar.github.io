Question 1

In what year did the Berlin Wall fall?

Correct Answer: 1989

The Berlin Wall fell on November 9, 1989, marking the beginning of the end of the Cold War and the reunification of Germany.

Question 2

Who was the first woman to win a Nobel Prize?

Correct Answer: Marie Curie

Marie Curie won the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1903 for her work on radioactivity, and later won the Nobel Prize in Chemistry in 1911.
